Kheer

Description

Kheer is a very common and tasty Indian dessert dish. Its rich and creamy texture makes it enjoyable for everyone at any occation. Originally from North India, its simple handful of ingredients make it easy for anyone for anyone to cook and enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Rice, ideally basmati rice: 1 cup.
  • Milk, ideally whole milk: 4 cups.
  • Sugar, 4 to 6 tablespoons

Steps:

  1. Wash the rice and let them soak in water for 30 mins.
  2. Add milk to a pot and let it boil.
  3. Once boiling, add soaked rice.
  4. keep stirring at a low to midium flame.
  5. Cook til the rice are soft, check by crushing with spoon if its mushy
  6. Add sugar.
  7. Lower the flame til it thickens.
  8. Once its thick and runny, shut off the heat.
  9. Let it cool and enjoy!
