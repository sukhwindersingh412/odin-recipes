CHA Recipe!

Description

You just had a bad day...or the complete opposite, the best day ever. you know what will make it better/complete? A nice cup of Cha! Cha is the single best thing in the world. I would say a soul is iincomplete without Cha. Cha also mean cha, which translates to desire, and the only cha a Cha drinker can have is Cha.

Ingredients

  • Water: 1/2 cup
  • Milk: 1/2 cup
  • Cardamom: 2 cushed pods
  • Cloves: 2
  • Fennel Seeds: a pinch
  • Ginger (Optional): per taste
  • Black tea (or loose tea equivalent): 2 Teaspoons
  • Sugar: per taste

Steps:

  1. Pour water in to a bowl with the spices and let boil.
  2. Add tea bag and let it boil for 2 minutes.
  3. Once the water changer to a dark color, add milk.
  4. Add sugar per taste.
  5. Pour into a cup and enjoy!
